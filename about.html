Our Story

Jain Saree Center has been a cornerstone of the textile industry in Malkapur for years. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted names in wholesale textiles. Our commitment to quality, competitive pricing, and customer satisfaction has been the foundation of our success.

We understand the needs of retailers and work tirelessly to provide them with the best products at the most competitive wholesale prices. Our extensive experience in the textile industry allows us to source the finest materials and deliver exceptional value to our customers.

Why Wholesale?

Jain Saree Center operates exclusively on a wholesale model. This allows us to:

  • Offer the most competitive prices in the market
  • Maintain consistent quality across large quantities
  • Build long-term partnerships with retailers
  • Provide dedicated support for bulk orders
  • Ensure reliable inventory for your business needs

Note: All products are sold in sets (4, 5, or 6 pieces per set depending on the product category). Minimum order is 1 set per product.
